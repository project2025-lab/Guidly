📦 Samsung Microwave — Quick Start Guide
1

Plug the microwave into a grounded power outlet and place it on a flat, stable surface with 4 inches of space on all sides.

2

Press the Clock button, enter the current time using the number pad, then press Clock again to confirm.

3

Place your food in a microwave-safe container. Press the Power Level button to select heat intensity (10 = full power).

4

Enter cook time using the number pad and press Start. The turntable will rotate your food for even heating.
